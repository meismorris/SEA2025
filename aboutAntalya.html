Dive into one of Europe’s largest aquarium complexes, where marine life and adventure unite.

Antalya Aquarium is one of the largest and most captivating aquarium complexes in Europe. Situated in the sunny Mediterranean city of Antalya, Turkey, it opened in 2012 and has since become a favorite destination for both tourists and locals. The facility offers an expansive and immersive underwater experience, bringing visitors face-to-face with thousands of sea creatures in carefully recreated marine ecosystems.

Its most iconic feature is the world’s longest tunnel aquarium, stretching 131 meters (430 feet) and surrounded by crystal-clear views of sharks, stingrays, and colorful fish. Beyond the tanks, the complex also includes themed attractions like Snow World and the Ice Museum, offering guests a chance to go from tropical reefs to icy wonderlands in a single visit.

Antalya Aquarium is more than just a marine exhibit—it's an interactive educational center. Through its marine biology labs, touch pools, and digital experiences, the aquarium encourages visitors to learn about conservation, the dangers of pollution, and the importance of preserving aquatic life for future generations. It's an unforgettable blend of fun, science, and environmental awareness.
