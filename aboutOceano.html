Europe's largest aquarium, Oceanogràfic blends organic forms with futuristic design to create an immersive underwater experience.

Oceanogràfic, located in Valencia, Spain, is Europe's largest aquarium and a landmark of contemporary marine architecture. Designed by Félix Candela, its dramatic shell-like roof and organic curves reflect the fluidity of the ocean itself.

Inside, visitors explore global marine habitats beneath soaring glass domes, surrounded by immersive, thoughtfully designed spaces that echo the beauty of the natural world.
